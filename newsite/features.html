Features

Here all features of our system has.

Remote Keyless Entry

1. Secure Encryption

Remote keyless entry via Sofia is securely encrypted to ensure only authorized users can unlock your doors. If a guest or visitor does not have access to your space, your smartphone becomes your secure, remote virtual key to allow them into your space, no matter where you are in the world.

2. Unlock over 4G or WiFi

Remote entry is easy with Sofia because you don't need Bluetooth or NFC to unlock your door. Unlocks can be authorized over 4G or WiFi to make sure that no matter where you are in the world, you can count on Sofia's remote keyless entry to work. Say goodbye to the days of having to make a special trip back to the office on the weekends to let in the cleaning staff or delivery man.

3. Advanced Reporting

When you're away from your office, you can still monitor access to your doors from the Sofia remote access dashboard. Anytime a door is unlocked, you will be able to track exactly who entered your space and at what time, giving you insight and data into the times of the day your office is most frequently used.

Unlock Data

1. Unlocks by User

With Sofia unlock data, you can see exactly when an authorized unlock happens and by whom the unlock is triggered. If you're having a delivery dropped off with a Sofia smartphone key, you can see exactly when it is delivered.

2. Real-time reports

Sofia unlock data automatically updates when a new unlock is detected on any of your doors. This type of interactive monitoring is perfect for virtual front desks and leadership that still wants to see what is happening in the office while remote. Our data also gives insight into when your doors are seeing the most foot traffic.

3. Detect Suspicious Unlocks

When an unlock occurs during non-office hours, you'll be able to see who performed the unlock in the Sofia data dashboard. You can also set notifications in the access logs to ensure you can detect this behavior in real-time. If you believe an unauthorized unlock happened, you can immediately revoke access and notify the appropriate security personnel.

Key Management

1. Group Key Profiles

Not all key holders should have the same access in your office or building. With group key profiles you can set restrictions on a group level to know that the right personnel can access the right places. For example, an IT group might be the only group with access to the IT room or an intern group might only have access from 9-6pm Monday through Friday.

2. Instantly Issue Keys

Keys can be instantly issued to an email address from the Sofia mobile apps available on Apple and Android. The opposite is also possible; keys can be instantly revoked from a user who may have left the company. No more keys floating in space or changing the locks when an employee parts ways with your organization.

3. See Key Usage Data

From the Sofia data dashboard you can see when keys are used and on what doors providing a 360 view of access to your space. You can nail down how often an employee is coming/going, if any keys are used during off hours, and even which doors have heaviest traffic and at what times.

Bluetooth Lock Connectivity

1. Bluetooth Compatible

Sofia unlocks occur when the mobile device is connected to the lock over Bluetooth signal. If key holders are required to connect over Bluetooth, they must have it powered on their device. Once on, the device will only recognize locks connected to Sofia and in tethering range of the mobile device. Once paired with the lock, the unlock signal will be sent once the distance restriction is met.

2. Secure Authentication

Securing a lock with Bluetooth will allow only keyholders in range of the beacon to unlock the door. This type of key holder restriction prevents employees from unlocking the door from anywhere. These key holders must be in range of the beacon for Sofia to authenticate the device and unlock the door.

3. Hands-free unlock

When a user is in range of the lock and has cleared the Bluetooth and distance restrictions, the unlock will occur with no added action from the user. No need to open the app or fumble through your wallet: simply approach the door and Sofia will recognize your device and unlock the door, all within seconds.

Two-Factor Authentication

1. Location Restrictions

Adding location restrictions to smartphone keys requires users to be within range of the beacon to send an unlock signal to the Sofia door controller. With this restriction in place, users must log in with their password and be within a specified range of the lock to unlock the door and access your office.

2. Unique phone pairing

All Sofia keys are teathered to a unique device that requires the key holder to be logged into the Sofia app on their device to unlock the door. This type of two-factor authentication prevents unauthorized logins when login information is compromised. Devices and users must be recognized by the Sofia system to unlock the door.

3. Suspicious Detection

When Sofia recognizes a key that attempts to unlock the door outside of their designated time or location, Sofia will notify the administratotr of such activities. This type of suspicious detection adds a layer of security to your two factor authentication to reinforce restrictions placed on keys and give the admin an unprecedented view into who is accessing their space and when.

Mobile Access Credentials

1. Secure Mobile Credentials

Download the Sofia App onto your phone and transform it into a mobile credential using secure, encrypted tokens to unlock the door. Any activity is audited in real time so BYOD and incident response for your physical security becomes an easy operational process.

2. Access via Bluetooth or NFC

With iPhones, Sofia’s bluetooth technology to is used to authenticate an unlock via the Sofia reader. Using Bluetooth low energy (BLE), your phone sends an encrypted signal to trigger the unlock of the door. Use Sofia’s near-field technology with Android phones to unlock doors via Sofia's NFC credentialing technology.

3. Mobile Two-Factor Authentication

Use the biometrics readers embedded on modern smartphones to authenticate at special security access points like IT rooms, data center doors, or access requests in off-hours that seem to be an anomaly. This prevents identity theft using biometric user authorization and allows you to run modern key management with ease.

Time Restrictions

1. Time of Day

When you restrict keys by time of day, users can only access the locks during a specificed time frame. This time restriction is especially useful if you have dedidcated office hours or part-time employees that should only have access to your office at certain times. For example, the cleaning staff might have keys that work from 6pm-9pm while full-time employees have access from 9am-6pm.

2. Day of the week

Day of the week time restrictions can help you secure your office on the weekends. If you restrict keys to only work Monday through Friday, you can be sure that no employees are coming in on the weekends. Combine with the time of day time restriction and full-time employees may only have valid keys Monday-Friday, 9am-6pm.

3. Date Expirations

Once a keys time restrictions have passed or expired, the key will expire and will not work for all locks in your office or building. This is especially useful when working with consultants, temporary works, or interns. It allows for a seemless offboarding process with no need to track down keys or change locks when a temporary employee finishes their time with the company.

Door Access Security Audit

1. Automatic audit reports

If you have a security audit coming up, waiting for the auditor to stop by and give you a list with insufficient items can cost you the license. Keep your access logs on file and pull unlock and access reports on demand, download as excel or csv and export it. This is crucial for financial services, medical, biotech or even non profits.

2. Security compliance license

HIPAA, ISO 27000 or SOC 2 type 2 require audited physical security in place. Sofia’s fully tracked user access system allows to pull events even for historic activities in the past. Answer questions like "Do past employees still have keys/access cards to the building?” or “Have past employees/ terminated employees been removed from having access to the property?” with ease.

3. Visitor Access Log

Relying solely on printed badges can be prone to errors. Check-in and check-out visitors to create a controlled access point for not only employees with keycards or swipe cards but for everyone getting access to the office. Keep a digital access log on file and even track check-ins when you are not there.

Digital Key Cards

1. Remote Access

Digital key cards are perfect for people who are constantly on the go. Our control access system allows you to unlock doors no matter where you are. With Sofia, you never have to worry about missing another package because you are away from home. You can simply unlock doors to guests straight from your mobile device.

2. Secure Unlocks

The strength of your building's security system can depend solely on the organization's IT infrastructure. With digital key cards, you can issue keys to guests for a set amount of time and have full control of who has access simply from one dashboard.

3. No unauthorized key duplications

Unauthorized key duplication can often be an issue to some companies and buildings. WIth our digital key cards, you never have to worry about your keys falling into the wrong hands as access to your building comes directly from one source, your phone.

Secure Wifi Unlocks

1. Instant Unlock

Having doors instantly unlock through wifi is extremely beneficial in that it saves the individual tons of time from fumbling around their keychain trying to search for the correct key. Traditional keys and door locks can often require maintenance through conventional wear and tear. Key Cards can often be faulty as readers do not always scan the cards correctly resulting in failed unlocks. Our system allows doors to be unlocked through wifi therefore resulting in a much more efficient process.

2. Authenticated in the cloud

Having your door authenticated through a cloud based system means ensures your building is safe and secure. We have designed our system so that the door reader and your phone transmit signals to each other for verification of information. With our cloud-based system, you can control who has access to your building, and if the information does not match, the door can not be opened. Our cloud based system ensures security to your building.

3. Control from anywhere

Another benefit of secure wifi unlocks is that you can have access to multiple doors from one location. This is especially convenient for business owners who have multiple offices dispersed around the globe. Our access control system notifies you of any suspicious activity and allows you to monitor and control multiple doors directly from one dashboard.

Multi-Location Access Management

1. Remote Monitoring

Successful key management is critical for any company. With Sofia, you can control and monitor access to your building directly from one dashboard. Ensure security by monitoring every entry or exit to your building and we will send you notifications of any suspicious activity.

2. Manage Access from anywhere

Sofia makes it convenient for people who are constantly on the go. Never have to you worry about missing another package again because you are not physically present at that time. With Sofia, you can unlock doors no matter where you are in the world.

3. Visitor Key Management

Visitor Key Management is the process in which you authorize keys to visitors for access to your building. Traditional ways of key management can often be dangerous as it is easy for visitors to copy or lose their keys. With Sofia, you never have to worry about unauthorized key duplications or misplacement of keys. You can issue keys to visitors and set time limitation on when they have access.

Remote Garage Gate Opening and Access Controller App

1. Remote Unlock

Visitors or service staff arrives at a locked gate or garage when you are not there. Use Sofia’s mobile Android or iOS controller app to remotely unlock the electric gate or garage when you can not be there in person. Learn how it works

2. Installed indoors
