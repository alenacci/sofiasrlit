Smart Check-In and Smart Check-Out

Sofia Jago augments existing hotels Property Management Systems (PMS) with the Self-Checkin and Self-Checkout service. With a Self-Checkin service, the hotel can offer a better service to its customers while reducing the management direct cost.


The Self-Checkin case study

  1. The hotel guest books a room
  2. The reservation is inserted within the PMS
  3. The PMS generates a VirtualKey using Sofia Jago
  4. The VirtualKey is sent to the guest
  5. The guest, using her smartphone, can enter the room directly, skipping over the hotel reception
  6. The app remains installed on the smartphone even after the leave: untapped marketing opportunities arise.


Easier AirBnb Hosting

AirB&B apartments are becoming the preferred accommodation choice. Sofia Otello and Sofia Jago can lead innovation in the management of such spaces. Private owners of accommodation facilities can ease their management process thanks to Sofia Jago and Sofia Otello: self-checkin for their guests and remote access grant and revocation for the housekeeper is now possible.


Ease your AirB&B guest management

  1. Mary’s is a frequent traveler and often put her house on AirB&B while she's out
  2. While she’s out, she can send a VirtualKey to a new host Over-The-Air using the Sofia Otello app.
  3. The guest receive the VirtualKey on her smartphone and can enter Mary’s house without any real key
  4. After the guest stay is over, Mary’s ask his housekeeper to clean the house and send to him another VirtualKey using the Otello app
  5. The housekeeper can enter the house directly with her smartphone


Enable smart working within your office

The way we work changed a lot in the last years. Also our workplaces are following this huge transformation. Sofia Jago lies lets you innovate the way to manage your business places. Smart workings means more productivity having more comfort at the same time. Your collaborators can manage their working spaces using their smartphone.


No more badges to enter restricted areas.

  1. John’s office has ISEO Argo and Sofia Jago; all the doors are now smart without any wired plant
  2. John needs to enter in a new meeting
  3. John opens the building management app on his smartphone
  4. He sends a VirtualKey request to the building manager
  5. The building manager, from his smartphone, authorises John to enter to room Over-The-Air


New business models for co-working spaces

Co-working spaces are becoming the preferred way young companies starts their business thanks to the possibility to scale costs with respect to the current space needs. Thanks to Sofia Jago, co-working can change the way they rent their spaces introducing a new as-a-service business model. Different subscriptions let you to enter different areas of the building, increasing the owner revenues.


Virtual Key required to benefit from specific services.

  1. John’s coworking space offers a cafeteria as a value added services to its premium customers
  2. The doors of John’s coworking feature ISEO Argo smart door locks
  3. As a premium user, John can access the cafeteria, while others are required to pay the premium fee to enter it, all from the same coworking’s app.


Sofia to enable a new frontier for logistic and delivery

Products delivery and logistic is becoming an essential tile in our everyday life. We buy interesting stuff on Amazon and similar services, waiting their delivery at our place. Thanks to Sofia Jago, the delivery business can be revolutionised, providing to customers an innovative in-home-delivery service. Value added service with reduced shipping costs.


Virtual Key required to simplify the delivery process

  1. Andy bought a new iPhone on amazon.com as a present for her fiancé birthday
  2. He has to receive the phone in 24h, otherwise he will be late for birthday party but unfortunately nobody can receive the delivery during the daytime (work time!)
  3. Thanks to Sofia, the delivery company that integrates Jago in its processes, can do a in-home-delivery Andy’s iPhone, entering the apartment and leaving the product on Andy’s table.


Sofia simplifying in-home health-care

Elder people need supportive health care provided in the home. Health Care has to be provided by professionals that needs to enter the home. Thanks to Sofia Jago, the in-home services like medical care can be simplified: dedicated health care professionals can enter the home without having the physical home keys, increasing the security of your domestic environment.


Virtual Key required to simplify the home keys management

  1. Auntie has a disease and needs home care assistance every day. Her sons cannot be present every medication since they have to be at workAuntie’ sons device to ask to MedicalCare Inc. to provide the needed assistance to their motherMedicalCare integrates Jago: this fact lets Auntie’s sons to let MedicalCar professional to enter the home only during the medication time without the need to provide the physical home-key to their employees.


Reducing infrastructures management costs

Your electrical, TLC or railway infrastructures have tons of small cabins all around the country. Managing the access for the needed maintenance to such cabins is a nightmare. Sofia solves all your issues.



New interactions for ATM points

Banks can benefits of Sofia Jago in two different ways: simplifying the management operations (i.e., key delivery for cleaning personnel) or by introducing the key delivery into their e-banking mobile application to enable safe and secure ATM cash withdrawal.
