Frequently asked questions

These are some of the most common things people ask us when they are considering mobile access for their office or building.

Is Sofia secure?

Yes, Sofia id secure. And it is more secure than most keycards and traditional keys. We use the same technology used by banks to protect the on-line accounts. Moreover, fingerprints can be used to get the highest level of security.

What happens if power or internet is out?

Our locks are battery powered and they have local memory, so they work also without power and without internet. Moreover, we have also standard keys as backup option for emergency cases. You will always have a way to access your space.

Can a key be used from any phone?

We support both Android and iOS. Once a cellphone is authorized, it can be used to open a door. If a device is stolen, that device can be disabled.


Do I have to change all my doors?

No, Sofia can be deployed on existing doors. Using the hardware produced by our partner ISEO, existing doors can be adapted to become smart. Once the smart lock is installed, your door is ready to be used with our software system.
