About us

Unlock new opportunities hidden behind the doors of your business.

Smartphone Based and Wire-Free
Access Control Platforms.

People are ever more willing to use their smartphone to carry over everyday activities. Access control is still based on pre-smartphone technologies. Keys, RFID and wire-based access control systems are the past. Sofia addresses these market needs by developing, integrating and commercialising distributed infrastructures for modern, mobile based and cost effective control access systems.

Sofia SRL is a subsidiary of Bottega52 SRL, a company operating since 2014 in the Internet Of Things market. Turnover in 2016 will be ~300 k€. Sofia SRL is currently funded by TIM (Telecom Italia Group), supported by Cisco by means of the Cisco Spark Innovation Fund and by Microsoft with the BizSpark Plus fund. Finally, ad deep industrial partnership is running with ISEO Serrature Spa.

Sofia SRL aims at innovating the Access Control Systems market, leveraging the access to high profile partners and the in-house technological competences.
