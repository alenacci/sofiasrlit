Features

Here all the features our system has.

Remote Access Management

1. Secure Encryption

Sofia ensure a secure encryption for the generated keys: in this way, only authorized users can unlock your doors. If a guest has to enter your place, you can send him a virtual key using your smart-phone thought the Internet on a certified, authenticated and cyphered communication channel.

2. Remote unlock via Internet

Using our gateway, you can unlock your door remotely, no matter where you are in the world. No more days with you doing a trip back to the office on the weekend to let enter the cleaning staff or delivery man.

3. Remote Access Log

While you are away from your place, you can monitor the access to your door using your smart-phone or your desktop computer thanks to our Access Log: when a door is unlocked, you will track exactly who entered your space and when.

4. Multiple Buildings

Sofia can manage multiple buildings remotely, directly from the mobile app or the remote dashboard.

Integrated Payments & Push Platform

1. Payment platform

We integrate PayPal as payment platform. It is really easy for you to sell the access to the buildings you are managing. We enable the possibility to easily managing your short-therm-rental. This is perfect for your Hotel, B&B, Room Music, Gym, etcâ€¦

2. Push notifications

We integrate also push notifications to your guests smart-phones. This feature enables your to reach your customers via Push Notifications to sell them access to special placing within your business. Are you an hotel manager and do you want to up-sell the Tennis Field? Send a push notification to your customer proposing them to buy a new entrance.

Remote Access Log

1. Unlocks by User

Using our Remote Access Log, you can see the exact time and date when an authorized unlock happens and by whom the unlock is triggered. The Remote Access Log contains also the list of the unauthorized unlock attempts.

2. Real-time access reports

When a door is unlocked, you can see within the Remote Access Log an entry with the event time-stamp and the user making the request. This feature is essential for virtual front desks and leadership that still wants to see what is happening within the managed place while remote.

3. Detect Suspicious Unlocks

If an unlock occurs during non-office hours, you'll be able to see who performed the unlock in the Sofia data Remote Access Log.

Key Management

1. Group Key Profiles

You can create user groups to manage similar personnel access rights. For example, you can create a group of the IT guys, giving the, access only to the IT room.

2. Instantly Issue and Revoke Keys Remotely

With Sofia, you can instantly issue or revoke keys to an email address from the Sofia mobile apps available on Apple and Android. In this way, you will avoid keys floating in space or changing the locks when an employee parts ways with your organization.

3. See Key Usage Data

From the Sofia data Remote Access Log you can see when keys are used and which door are mostly used. In this way you can optimize how your place is used by your personnel.

Bluetooth Lock Connectivity

1. Bluetooth Compatible

Sofia integrated the smart locks manufactured by ISEO, a worldwide locks manufactures. Their smart locks are Bluetooth 4.0 enabled and they are compatible with most of the smart-phones today available. No pairing is required: just open the app and press the OPEN button to lock unlock the door.

2. Security

Since Bluetooth is a proximity technology, key-holders can unlock the door only while they are in range of the lock radio signal. This ensure an higher level of security since only who his in place can enter the door.

Mobile Credentials

1. Secure Mobile Credentials

Download the Sofia App on your smartphone and use it to open your locks: you can transform your phone in a secure door mobile credential: we use encrypted tokens and elliptic curves with OTP technology to make it the most secure system.

2. Unlock via Bluetooth or NFC

Thanks to the ISEO locks, we use Sofiaâ€™s Bluetooth technology to unlock a door using the smart-phone. Using Bluetooth low energy (BLE), your phone sends an encrypted signal to lock. NFC is available to be used via smart cards, as backup for your smart-phone.

3. Mobile Fingerprints Authentication

We use the smart-phone fingerprint sensor as an additional authentication factor to ensure special security access. In this way you can be sure about the person entering your place.

Time Restrictions

1. Time of Day

Keys can be restricted by time of day: in this way users can only access the locks during a specified time frame. This kind of restrictions are useful when you have dedicated office hours or part-time employees that should only have access to your place at certain times (for example, the cleaning staff).

2. Day of the week

It is possible to setup keys time restrictions with respect to the day of the week, for instance to secure your place on the weekends. If you restrict keys to only work Monday through Friday, you can be sure that no one can enter in the weekends. Combining the rule with the time of day, your personnel can only have valid keys from Monday to Friday, from 9am to 6pm.

3. Date Expirations

Every key has its own date expiration: once a key is expired, it will not work for all locks in your office or building. This is useful when you have temporary workers, consultants, or interns since no do not need to track down keys or change locks when a temporary employee finishes their time with your organization.
